About

A slower companion to the portfolio.

This blog holds a wider kind of writing than a portfolio can. It is where work notes, technical lessons, travel reflections, and everyday observations can live side by side without needing to fit one strict category.

Why this site exists

Writing that stays useful after the moment passes

The blog is meant to stay readable, open-ended, and practical as the range of topics grows.

I work in engineering operations, which means I spend a lot of time thinking about coordination, support systems, documentation, logistics, and the invisible work that helps teams move well. Those topics naturally belong here, but I also want the blog to stay open to other interests.

Some posts will be practical and work-related. Some will be more reflective. Others may come from travel, tools, websites, process design, or something small that feels worth keeping. The common thread is simple: if an idea helps me understand something better, I want to give it a clear place to live.
